How to Play

🏰 The Goal
Enemies march down the path toward your castle. Place turrets to stop them! If too many get through, your castle falls.
🔫 Turrets
Left-click a green tile to place a turret. Click an existing turret to upgrade it (up to Lv.5). Right-click a turret to sell it for half price. Shift+click to view stats.
⚔️ 8 Turret Types
🔫 Blaster — steady DPS • 🎯 Sniper — long range • 💣 Bomber — area splash • 🧊 Freezer — slows enemies • 🔴 Laser — rapid fire • ⛓️ Chain — bounces between enemies • ☠️ Poison — stacking damage over time • 🩸 Cursed — 3x power but drains castle HP
👾 Enemy Types
Normal — standard • ⚡ Fast — quick but fragile • 🛡️ Shield — extra HP • 👑 Boss — massive HP, special abilities • 🧨 Bomber — stuns turrets on death • 💚 Healer — heals nearby enemies • 🌀 Teleporter — blinks forward • 💥 Splitter — splits into 3 on death
💡 Tips
• Every 5th wave is 🌙 Night Mode — turrets act as flashlights
• Every 10th wave is ⚡ Elite — enemies glow gold, move faster, drop 3x coins
• Double-tap the wave counter for 2x/3x speed
• ☄️ Meteor Strike and ⏳ Time Freeze abilities have cooldowns — use them wisely
• Earn coins to buy skins, traps, and screen effects in the shops
• Reach wave 20+ and prestige for permanent bonuses
🕯️ Secrets
There are 24 hidden secrets and Easter eggs. Check the ? Whispers button on the menu for cryptic clues. Some involve typing words, some involve clicking things repeatedly, and some only appear at certain times...
